# Chronos Fusion v2.5 rulebook

## Full Chronos qualification

A fixture enters the full model only when:

- Same-league historical sample is at least 120 matches
- Each team has at least eight historical matches before kickoff
- Home and away venue samples contain at least four matches each
- Required market odds exist
- At least three of four engines approve the selection
- Published odds are 1.19 or higher

A Banker additionally requires:

- Full tier only
- Four of four engines pass
- Strict candidate checks pass
- Confidence is at least 82
- Positive market edge
- Odds are at least 1.19

## Provisional global-odds qualification

Used only when full qualification cannot be completed.

- Complete Home/Draw/Away odds are required
- Only the stronger home or away side is considered
- Published odds must be between 1.19 and 2.15
- Market fair probability must show a clear favourite
- At least 120 closely matched historical 1X2 profiles are required
- Historical hit rate, price similarity, clear-favourite strength and data quality must pass
- Three of four engines must approve
- Athena remains failed because local team intelligence is incomplete
- Provisional selections are always Medium risk
- Provisional selections can never be Bankers

## Low-odds rule

When a full selection is below 1.19, Chronos tests the next stronger related market. It publishes the upgrade only when the stronger market independently passes strict checks. When no stronger priced market is available, the match is rejected.

For provisional 1X2 selections, no stronger visible market is available from the listing feed, so a price below 1.19 is rejected rather than replaced with an invented price.
